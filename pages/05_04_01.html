Post-Transplant Bloodwork and Management:

Clinical Visits:

  • Weekly × 2 weeks → every 2 weeks × 1 month → monthly × 6 months → every 3 months × 6–12 months → every 6–12 months long-term.

Routine Blood Work

  • AST, ALT, ALP, bilirubin, CBC, creatinine, Na, K, Mg, phosphate, INR, tacrolimus levels.
  • Twice weekly × 2 weeks → weekly × 1 month → every 2 weeks × 6 months → monthly × 6 months → every 3 months thereafter.

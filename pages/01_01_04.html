Donor Compensation and Support

Inform living donors that may be able to get reimbursed for expenses incurred for organ donation. This is through expense reimbursement from Trillium Gift of Life Network (TGLN).

The following forms need to be completed:

  • PRELOD Application Form
  • Income and Benefit Verification Form
  • Loss of Income Certificate
  • Travel Support Letter*

*Completed by Transplant Coordinator/Surgeon to assist with travel during transplant assessment process

 

For out of province/country or rural transplant donors, recipients and family members travelling for transplant related reasons, they can be reimbursed from HopeAir.  

Donor from Manitoba can also complete an LORDP Application Form

In addition, inform patients that they can claim for medical expenses for the CRA from the following guide.

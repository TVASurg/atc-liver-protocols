General work up and Considerations Diseased Donors

Blood-work 

General: ABO typing and crossmatch, AST, ALT, ALP, bilirubin, sodium, potassium

Infectious Disease: Blood and urine cultures, CMV, EBV, HIV, HTLV, HBV, HCV, syphilis (TPPA), toxoplasmosis serologies  

Region-Specific: West Nile Virus (WNV), Strongyloides, Trypanosoma serologies 

Imaging   CT chest /Abdomen/ Pelvis.

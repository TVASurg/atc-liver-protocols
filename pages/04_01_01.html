Pre-Operative (Blood work and Prep):

Blood work-up:

  • CBC
  • Electrolytes
  • Ca, Mg, Phosphate
  • Liver Enzymes: AST, ALT, ALP
  • INR
  • Bilirubin
  • COVID NPS
  • CXR
  • ECG  
  • CT Chest/abdomen (For HCC is no imaging within 3M)
  • AFP (For HCC is no result within 6M)
  • HBV DNA (for HBV positive patients)

Diet: DAT followed by NPO from Midnight to 6 hours before OR time

Medications:

  • Methylprednisolone 500mg IV once 30-60minutes before the surgery
  • Cefazolin 1g IV once 30-60minutes before the surgery.  
  • Vancomycin 1g IV once 30-60 minutes if allergic to Beta lactam or known MRSA before the surgery

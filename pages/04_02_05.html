Medications (Anti-fungal and Anti-parasitic Prophylaxis):

Nystatin

  • Indicated in all patients not requiring systemic antifungal.
  • Dose: 1 mL TID × 3 months, then BID × 3 months, then stop.

Fluconazole

  • Indicated in the following patients:
    • Choledochojejunostomy.
    • Return to OR within 5 days post-OLT.
    • Re-transplant.
    • Pre-op Cr >177 or HD within 48h pre-op.
    • Massive transfusion (>40 units blood, 1 week only).
    • Candida colonization or iron overload.
  • Dose: 400 mg PO daily × 4 weeks.
  • Lifelong if positive coccidioidomycosis serology

Micafungin

  • Indicated if fluconazole indication + azole resistance/allergy.
  • Dose: 50 mg IV daily × 4 weeks.

Voriconazole

  • Indicated in the following:
    • Prior invasive aspergillosis.
    • Invasive non-albicans Candida.
    • Scedosporium apiospermum growth.
  • Dose: 200 mg PO BID × 3–6 months.

Ivermectin

  • Indicated in those with Positive strongyloidiasis serology.
  • Dose: 200 mcg/kg/day × 2 days.

Imaging Surveillance:

  • HCC (RETREAT score based)
    • Score 0–3: CT chest/abdomen + AFP q6 months × 2 yrs
    • Score 4: Same × 5 yrs + consider sirolimus/minimize IS
    • Score ≥5: CT/AFP q3 months × 2 yrs → q6 months × 2–5 yrs + consider sirolimus/minimize IS
  • Cholangiocarcinoma:
    • Mayo protocol: MRI abd + CT chest + CA19-9 q3–6 months lifelong.
    • Incidental: MRI abd + CT chest + CA19-9 q3 months × 2 yrs → q6 months × 3 yrs → annually lifelong
  • PSC: MRCP q2–3 yrs.
  • Fibroscan: All patients: q1–2 yrs.

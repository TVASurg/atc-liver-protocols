Osteoporosis/Osteopenia

  • Workup: TSH, PTH, anti-TTG, testosterone, Vit D, Ca, phosphate, thoracolumbar X-ray
  • General: Vit D 1000 IU daily, Ca 500 mg PO BID, weight-bearing exercise
  • Osteopenia (BMD –1 to –2.5): If progression/fracture → Risedronate 35 mg PO weekly OR Prolia 60 mg SC q6 mo if bisphosphonate fails/intolerant
  • Osteoporosis (BMD <–2.5, atraumatic fracture, or osteopenia + risk factors): Same as above + osteoporosis clinic referral

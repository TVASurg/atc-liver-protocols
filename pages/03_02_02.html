Smoking Cessation:

  • Strongly recommended to stop smoking prior to admission on Waitlist due to increased risk of cancer under immunosuppression after liver transplantation
  • Patients with clinically relevant lung disease and cardiovascular disease must stop smoking before they can be included on the waiting list.
  • Use the following to Monitor Recent Smoking:
    • Urinary: Urinary level of >50ng/ml , T1/2 23h
    • Serum cotinine levels: Serum level of >10ng/ml, T1/216h

Chronic Ductopenic Rejection

  • With acute T-cell component: IV methylprednisolone 500 mg × 3 days, maximize tacrolimus (trough 8–10), add myfortic
  • With normal liver function (MELD <15): Maximize tacrolimus (8–10), add myfortic
  • With abnormal liver function (MELD <15): Keep tacrolimus trough 4–6, relist for transplan

Living Donor Evaluation Process Overview:

1. Screening:

• Complete the Donor Health History Form and attach documentation of donor’s blood type

• Program will contact suitable donor for Preliminary assessment (i.e., Blood tests, ECG,Chest X-Ray)

2. A Full assessment will then be placed requiring the following:

• CT Scan

• Pulmonary Function Test

• MRI

• Liver Biopsy

• Psychological Assessment

• Additional Heart Tests (ECG, Exercise Stress test)

• Consultations for Weight Loss (as needed)

• Meetings with Anesthesiologist, social worker and independent medical team

For full assessment guidelines refer to Living Donor Liver Suitability Assessment LDL-003

3. Approval will occur after reviewing all test results and a meeting with donor team to determine donor eligibility

• Donor safety is top priority hence inform patients that if they risk is high, the donor team may reject them

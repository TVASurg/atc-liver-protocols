Gout:

Acute Flare:

  • 1st: Colchicine 1.2 mg once → 0.6 mg 1h later, then 0.6 mg q12–24h until resolved (if not on prednisone/diabetic)
  • OR Prednisone 30–40 mg PO daily × 5 days, taper by 5–10 mg/week

Prophylaxis Indications: ≥2 attacks/year, CKD, tophi, uric acid stones

  • Target uric acid <360
  • 1st line: Allopurinol 100 mg daily → titrate q2w + rheumatology referral
  • 2nd line: Febuxostat 40 mg daily → titrate q2w + rheumatology referral

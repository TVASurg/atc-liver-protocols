Referrals:

Referrals for Liver Transplant should be based on the following criteria for the different presentations:

1.   Chronic Liver Disease:

  •    Presence of ascites or hepatic hydrothorax
  •    Resolved spontaneous bacterial peritonitis (SBP)
  •    Hepatic encephalopathy
  •    Jaundice
  •    Portal hypertensive gastrointestinal bleeding
  •    Na MELD ≥ 11 or Child-Pugh B score ≥ 7
  •    Related complications: HPS, HCC

2.   Fulminant Hepatic Failure:

  •    Patients with rapid-onset liver failure and no prior liver disease.

3.   Metabolic Liver Disorders:

  •    Hereditary transthyretin amyloidosis
  •    Hyperoxaluria type I
  •    Other inherited hepatic conditions

Operative (Living Donor):

The surgical procedure for living liver donation is complex and requires detailed coordination across surgical, anesthetic, and perfusion teams. To ensure all team members are aligned on best practices and institutional standards, please refer to the following standard operating procedures (SOPs):

  • LDL-007 – Living Donor Liver Operative Protocol
  • LDL-009 – Living Donor Liver Perfusion and Packaging

These SOPs provide step-by-step guidance on intraoperative techniques, perfusion procedures, packaging, and transport protocols for the graft.

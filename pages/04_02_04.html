Medications (Viral Prophylaxis):

Valganciclovir / Ganciclovir

  • Indication for patients who are or received a CMV positive liver (i.e., D+/R- × 3 months, CMV D-/R+ or D+/R+ with ATG × 3 months)
  • Dose:
    • Valganciclovir: 900 mg PO daily (start 10–14 days post-LT).
    • Ganciclovir: 5 mg/kg IV daily if absorption concerns.
    • Renal-adjust per EGFR  

Acyclovir:

  • Indicated for patients not requiring CMV prophylaxis.
  • Dose:  
    • 400 mg PO BID × 1 month
    • Renal-adjust per EGFR  

Lamivudine / Tenofovir (TDF) / Entecavir

  • Indicated for the following:
    • HBV cirrhosis patients
    • Recipients with HBcAb+
    • HBV naïve receiving HBcAb+ graft
    • Any donor with HBsAg+
  • Dose:
    • Lamivudine: 100 mg PO daily (renal-adjust).
    • TDF: 300 mg PO daily (renal-adjust).
    • Entecavir: 0.5 mg PO daily (renal-adjust). 

HBIG

  • Indicated for the following:
    • HBV DNA >1000 pre-LT.
    • Unknown HBV DNA pre-LT (until results).
    • HBV with HDV co-infection
  • Dose
    • 10 mL IV during anhepatic phase, then daily × 7 days, weekly × 4 weeks, monthly × 1 year

Epclusa / Mavyret

  • Indicated for the following: Untreated HCV pre-LT, HCV-positive donor graft recipients.
  • Dose:
    • Epclusa (sofosbuvir 400 mg + velpatasvir 100 mg) × 12 weeks.
    • Mavyret (glecaprevir 300 mg + pibrentasvir 120 mg) with meals × 8 weeks.
    • Typically started at 3 months post-LT
